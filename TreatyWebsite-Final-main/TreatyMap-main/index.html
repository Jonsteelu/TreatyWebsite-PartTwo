Treaty One

Treaty One was part of the numbered treaties. It was signed on August 3, 1871, in Lower Fort Gary . It was a region inside manitoba which encompassed winnipeg. Seven indigenous tribes were signatories to the treaty and they compose the treaty one nation, as well as its governing council.
