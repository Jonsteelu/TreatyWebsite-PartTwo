Todays Implementation

One example of todays implementation of treaty one is the propossed "former Kapyong Barracks Master Plan." It is a plan for a large scale redevelopment of the land, and a "landmark project." It is meant as a plan to merge "the former Kapyong Barracks back into the urban fabric, creating new connections into the surrounding Tuxedo and River Heights neighbourhoods."


Information source: Treaty 1 Naawi-Oodena Master Plan
