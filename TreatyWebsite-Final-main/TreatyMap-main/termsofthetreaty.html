Terms Of Treaty One

Treaty One included many terms that were agreed between the Indigenous people and the Canadian Government Agents.

For terms that the Canadian government had to follow:
One term that was included was that each family of five was to recieve at least 160 acres of land. As well each band was to recieve a reserve large enough to provide this land for families. Another term was that each person was to recieve a one time payment of $3, and each family to recieve $15 every year. It was also promised that a school would be built and maintained by the government.
