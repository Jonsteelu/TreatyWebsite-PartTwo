Importance of Treaty One

Treaty One played a quite important role in the relation between the Canadian government and the indigienos people. Treaty One was the first treaty signed, setting up the stage for other treaties to be signed later. It was the first example of a agreement between Canada and indigenous people, and proved that negotiations and agreements could be made, to benefit the both.
